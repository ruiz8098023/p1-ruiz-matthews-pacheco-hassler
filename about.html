About

South Florida’s Comic Book, Anime, Animation, Video Game, Fantasy, Sci-Fi and Pop Culture Convention will return in 2021 to the Miami Beach Convention Center.

Supercon is days of fun featuring celebrity guests, comic book creators, voice actors, industry guests, cosplayers, artists, writers, panels, Q&A’s, films & shorts, costume & cosplay contests, vendors, parties, anime, workshops, video gaming and more.

Show Features

After Dark

Whether you want to sing your heart out at our late-night karaoke or just play some games and grab a drink with friends, the party doesn’t stop after dark.

Artist Alley

Supercon’s Artist Alley brings the artistic masterminds behind your favorite comic books, graphic novels, artwork, and more.

Cosplay Central

Cosplay Central is an area of our show floor devoted entirely to the art of costumes, crafts, and characters..

Family HQ

Kids and parents alike can join in our drawing workshops, interactive activities, and entertaining performances all weekend long in Family HQ.

Gaming Zone

Featuring organized play, tournaments, and freeplay for all the hottest new games, retro favorites and can’t-miss classics, this is the perfect place to take a short rest.

Pop Asia

Pop Asia has interactive experiences, gaming, local cultural partners, and anime & manga retailers. It’s like an anime convention in the heart of Supercon!
